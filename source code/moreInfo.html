Air Pollution Condition in Malaysia

Air pollution is a significant concern in Malaysia, affecting air quality in different regions of the country. Industrial activities, motor vehicle emissions, biomass burning, and other human activities contribute to high levels of air pollutants. Efforts are being made to address this issue through regulations and public awareness campaigns. By prioritizing clean technologies and sustainable practices, Malaysia aims to improve air quality and ensure a healthier environment for its population.
