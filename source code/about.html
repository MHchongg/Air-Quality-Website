Welcome to AirQualityMY

AirQualityMY is dedicated to providing up-to-date information on air pollution in Malaysia. It aims to raise awareness about the impact of air pollution on human health and the environment. Explore AirQualityMY to understand the sources of pollution, access real-time air quality data, and learn about the measures to combat this pressing issue.

Mission

The mission of AirQualityMY is to empower individuals, communities, and policymakers with accurate and reliable information about air pollution. It strives to foster a greater understanding of the causes and consequences of air pollution, enabling informed decisions and actions towards cleaner and healthier air for all.

Things to explore

  • Real-time Air Quality Data: Access the latest air quality index and pollutant levels for different regions in Malaysia. Stay informed about the current air quality status and take necessary precautions.
  • Causes of Air Pollution: To have a deeper understanding of air pollution, it is essential to explore its origins and contributing factors. Air pollution in Malaysia is influenced by a combination of natural occurrences and human activities. By examining these causes, we can comprehend the complexities of air pollution and its impact on the environment and public health.
  • Measures of Air Pollution: In order to combat air pollution effectively, implementing measures is crucial. These measures aim to reduce emissions from various sources, promote sustainable practices, raise awareness, and strengthen monitoring and enforcement, ensuring cleaner and healthier air for all.
  • Comparison to Other Countries: To know how good or bad is our country's air quality condition by comparing with other countries in South East Asia.
